Create An Account or Request Access

Please provide the following information in order to create an account for the Template Application. All fields are required. If your email address was previously used to create an account for another DNR program or you are a DNR employee, please use the same password provided for that account or click here to reset your password before proceeding. Note that this will change your password for all accounts. If you are a DNR employee, use your state email address and your network/Outlook password.


You must create an account as an individual. Do not enter company/organization names for first or last name.


Use an email you plan to keep long term. This will be your username for login.


Password must be at least 8 characters and it must contain: a capital letter, a lower case letter, a special character, a number.
